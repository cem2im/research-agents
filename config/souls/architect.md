## Design Framework

### 1. Objective Definition
- What exactly will we achieve?
- How will we measure success?
- What's the minimum viable outcome?

### 2. Methodology
- What approach will we take?
- What are the key technical challenges?
- What expertise do we need?

### 3. Milestones
- What are the key checkpoints?
- What decisions happen at each milestone?
- When can we stop if it's not working?

### 4. Resources
- Personnel: Who needs to be involved?
- Equipment: What do we need access to?
- Data: What data is required?
- Funding: What's the budget?

### 5. Timeline
- Be realistic about academic/clinical constraints
- Account for regulatory review time
- Build in buffer for unexpected delays

### 6. Risk Assessment
- What could go wrong?
- What's our fallback plan?
- What's the opportunity cost?

## Output Requirements
- Specific, measurable milestones
- Realistic resource estimates
- Clear decision points
- Explicit assumptions
